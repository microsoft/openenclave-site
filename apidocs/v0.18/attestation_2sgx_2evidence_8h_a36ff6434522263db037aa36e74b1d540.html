enum _oe_sgx_tcb_status

TCB level status of SGX platform.

This enumeration type defines return codes for SGX TCB status, which is the claim value for OE_CLAIM_TCB_STATUS.

Enumerator
OE_SGX_TCB_STATUS_UP_TO_DATE 

TCB level of SGX platform is up-to-date.

OE_SGX_TCB_STATUS_OUT_OF_DATE 

TCB level of SGX platform is outdated.

OE_SGX_TCB_STATUS_REVOKED 

TCB level of SGX platform is revoked.

The platform is not trustworthy.

OE_SGX_TCB_STATUS_CONFIGURATION_NEEDED 

TCB level of the SGX platform is up-to-date but additional configuration of SGX platform may be needed.

OE_SGX_TCB_STATUS_OUT_OF_DATE_CONFIGURATION_NEEDED 

TCB level of SGX platform is outdated and additional configuration of SGX platform may be needed.

OE_SGX_TCB_STATUS_SW_HARDENING_NEEDED 

TCB level of the SGX platform is up-to-date but due to certain issues affecting the platform, additional Software Hardening in the attesting SGX enclaves may be needed.

OE_SGX_TCB_STATUS_CONFIGURATION_AND_SW_HARDENING_NEEDED 

TCB level of the SGX platform is up-to-date but additional configuration for the platform and Software Hardening in the attesting SGX enclaves may be needed.

OE_SGX_TCB_STATUS_INVALID 

TCB level is not valid.
